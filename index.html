About Me
Anthony Schuller is a Montreal-based actor, best known for his portrayal of leading man Brad Majors in The Rocky Horror Show, the original stage production of the cult classic film, directed by Amy Blackmore and produced by Mainline Theatre. Most recently, Anthony played tormented teenager Curtis in Canadian playwright Jordan Tannahill's Late Company, directed by Dale Hayes and produced by d2 productions.

Graduate of the Dawson Professional Theatre program, Anthony is classically trained, and relies on a well-rounded foundation to support his present-day acting. In theatre school, he explored quintessential lover roles the likes of Lysander in Shakespeare's A Midsummer Night's Dream, and most notably performed as The Mechanic, a charming troubled youth and addict, in American playwright Will Eno's Middletown.
